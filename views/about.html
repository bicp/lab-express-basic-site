About Zico

Interesting facts about Zico, the Dog

  1. Zico, the Dog, was born on February 3rd, 2020.

    He was named after the famous Brazilian football coach and former player who played as an attacking midfielder, Arthur Antunes Coimbra, a.k.a Zico, born in March 3rd, 1973.

  2. He is a maltese dog

    The Maltese is gentle, affectionate, intelligent, responsive and trusting. A good family dog, Maltese are lively, playful, vigorous, and they generally enjoys learning tricks. They can be snappy with raucous children. The petite build of the Maltese belies its fearless presence. Highly alert, the Maltese elicits a flurry of barking in response to unfamiliar noises.

  3. Dogs may be able to fall in love with you.

    Thanks to science, you can know, for a fact, that your dog loves you back. A 2015 study at Azabu University in Japan found an uptick in the level of oxytocin in both dogs and their owners when they stare at each other.
